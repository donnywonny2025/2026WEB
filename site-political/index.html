About

Hi, I'm Jeff.

I'm a creative producer and editor based in Grand Rapids, Michigan. For the last fifteen years, I've specialized in high-stakes production across the political, advocacy, and corporate landscape. From rapid-response political ads and compliance-ready government communications to global New Balance campaigns, I deliver broadcast-quality narratives under the tightest deadlines.

I understand the tempo of a campaign cycle and the precision required for advocacy work. I edit, I animate, and I build the tools to tell your story. I work fast. I work clean. I don't miss air dates.
