About

Hi, I'm Jeff.

I'm a creative director, editor, and producer based in Grand Rapids, Michigan. For the last fifteen years I've been making things that move people — across entertainment, corporate, government, and political projects. From New Balance campaigns and NASA ceremonies to rapid-response political ads and Danny Trejo's TV show, I bring the same energy to every cut.

I edit. I animate. I build apps. I code. If it tells a story and needs to look great, I'm already interested. I work fast. I work clean. I don't miss deadlines.
