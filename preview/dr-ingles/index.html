Meet Dr. Paul

A dentist who feels
like family.

Dr. Paul grew up in Port Huron, Michigan and knew early on he wanted to take care of people. He studied at Liberty University in Virginia, where he met his high school sweetheart Taylor, and went on to earn his doctorate at Midwestern University in Illinois. While there he picked up the Excellence in Patient Care Award, which pretty much tells you everything about how he practices.

After a few years working in Burton, Dr. Paul and Taylor made the move to Wayland and took over the practice right here on Main Street. They wanted a place where they could put down roots, raise their kids, and build real relationships with the people they see every day. When he is not at the office, you will probably find him and Taylor out on the pickleball court, in the garden, or chasing their little ones around the yard.
