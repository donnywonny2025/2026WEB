# The White Rabbit: "Down The Rabbit Hole" Experience
**Status:** Foundation implemented (Rabbit icon in footers, blank HTML file deployed). Active development paused to prioritize job seeking.

## Core Concept
The "Down the Rabbit Hole" page (`down-the-rabbit-hole.html`) will act as an interactive, gamified easter egg for users who happen to find and click the tiny white rabbit icon `(\_/)` at the bottom right of the main and political portfolios.

## Design & UI Requirements
1. **Aesthetic**: Super slick, premium, dark-mode design matching the primary site's DNA. 
2. **Layout**: The core interactive elements will be self-contained inside a central "box" or terminal-like interface in the absolute center of the page.
3. **Tech Stack**: HTML/CSS/JS with potential React integration for complex state management and fluid micro-animations.

## The Gameplay Loop
The page will feature a series of escalating mini-games or puzzles the user must pass to advance deeper into the "rabbit hole".

**Level 1: The Foundation**
* **Game**: A highly polished, elevated version of Rock, Paper, Scissors.
* **Execution**: Needs top-level game theory logic under the hood (e.g., AI that predicts patterns if the user plays multiple rounds). 
* **Visuals**: Fun, interesting animations triggered on wins/losses/ties. Needs to feel incredibly premium, not like a cheap web game.

## Future Development Notes
- Keep the scope tight. Do not spend a "million years" building complex RPG mechanics. 
- Focus on high visual impact for low development effort (e.g., CSS glow effects, smooth transitions, satisfying sound design).
- Each passing level should reveal a piece of a larger narrative or unlock a secret portfolio/contact method.
